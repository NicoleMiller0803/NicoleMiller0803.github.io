Zara Homepage Redesign

In LSC 532, "Web Design for the Sciences," our mission was to revamp a homepage that posed challenges for end users. The primary objective was to acquaint ourselves with Adobe XD and adeptly translate its mockups into HTML and CSS code. Focused on simplicity and user-friendliness, my design aimed to embody Zara's mission statement: "Our website serves as a hub for teens and young adults, providing information on Zara's clothing offerings. It's designed to empower consumers, helping them expand their wardrobe and build confidence through fashion."

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, Adobe XD

Center for Native Peoples and the Environment Website Redesign

In LSC 532, "Web Design for the Sciences," we partnered with the Center for Native Peoples and the Environment at the State University of New York. Our goal: redesign their website for improved user-friendliness and information flow. Close collaboration with the client allowed us to understand design issues and navigate technological constraints. Despite time limitations, we produced a comprehensive Adobe XD design, though full website coding wasn't feasible. The design, while not fully implemented, stands as a strategic blueprint for future development, showcasing our commitment to innovative web design.
